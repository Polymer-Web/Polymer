Dice Rules (Section A)

  1. Rolling the die decides how many moves you have to go.
  2. You cannot move diagonally.
  3. Rolling a four makes you roll again
    1. Rolling a four twice forces you to move exactly once.
  4. Rolling a five is the same as rolling a two.
  5. Rolling a six makes you lose 2 of your turns
    1. Rolling a six on a purple square lets you win the game.

Starting Area (Section B)

  1. This is the starting place of the game.
  2. This is one of the few visible areas of the game, anybody inside the blue area is immune to orange tiles.

Automatons (Red Areaa) (Section C)

  1. If you run into a red area, go back to the blue tile.
    1. If you are in a yellow area, you are not affected by any red spaces.
    2. Red Spaces are shaped in a cross, not a 3x3 area.
